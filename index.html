MIDWEST.IO
November 9-10
Kansas City, MO
Midwest.io is a two-day conference in the heart of the US, bringing together developers for an eclectic collection of talks covering the latest trends, best practices, and research in the field of computing.

Register

Tickets are $249. Register via Eventbrite here.

Group discounts are now available! Receive 10% off 5+ tickets and 15% off 10+ tickets. Email team@midwest.io for details.

If you plan on staying at the Marriott during Midwest.io, check out our venue & hotel information for the special conference rate.

Please note that all attendees are expected to abide by our code of conduct.
