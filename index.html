MIDWEST.IO
July 14-15
Kansas City, MO
Midwest.io is a two-day conference in the heart of the US, bringing together developers for an eclectic collection of talks covering the latest trends, best practices, and research in the field of computing.

Venue

Kansas City Marriott Downtown

200 W 12th St, Kansas City, MO 64105

Call for Presentations

We're looking for awesome presentations for Midwest.io. As the focus of the conference is on latest trends, best practices, and research in technology, we're looking for talks that would fit into this theme. As we plan on picking an eclectic collection of talks, we're not looking for talks in a particular technology, language, or framework.

As a speaker, we value the time and effort you put into creating a great presentation. As such, we'll cover all of your registration, travel, and accomodations for the entire conference.

Submit your talk today! The call for papers will close April 29.
