MIDWEST.IO
August 22-23 2016
Kansas City, MO
Midwest.io is a two-day conference in the heart of the US, bringing together developers for an eclectic collection of talks covering the latest trends, best practices, and research in the field of computing.

Register

Registration is available! Early bird registration will be available until May 31st. See everyone at the conference! Register now.

Please note that all attendees are expected to abide by our code of conduct.

Call For Papers

Submit your talk today!

Midwest.io will cover all speaker costs including travel to and from Kansas City, hotel, transportation to and from the hotel, and registration for the conference.


The deadline for talk submissions is Sunday, April 30th.


This year, each of the talks will incorporate the theme of coping with chaos. Join us for two days of talks covering the explosion of data, complexity of distributed systems, emergence of security threats, and our reliance on connectivity.
